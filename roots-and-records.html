📲 Import & Export
Notability · Apple Notes · RTF · PDF · Plain Text — move your research anywhere
How This Works
📤 Export to Notability
Generates an RTF file with all your research. Open it in Notability via Files app or share sheet. Notability accepts RTF, PDF, and TXT natively.
📤 Export to Apple Notes
Copies formatted text to clipboard. On iPhone/iPad, open Notes → New Note → Paste. Or taps the notes:// URL to open Notes directly.
📥 Import from Either App
Paste text copied from Notability or Apple Notes. The importer reads structured text and pulls it into the right section of Roots & Records.

📥 IMPORT FROM NOTABILITY OR APPLE NOTES
How to Get Text Out of Those Apps
From Notability:
1. Open your note in Notability
2. Tap Share → Export as RTF or TXT
3. Open the file → Select All → Copy
4. Paste into the box below
From Apple Notes:
1. Open your note in Apple Notes
2. Select All text (⌘A or hold → Select All)
3. Copy (⌘C or Copy)
4. Paste into the box below
⚡ QUICK COPY — Individual Sections
📱 iOS Shortcut Tip — Send to Roots & Records with One Tap
Create an Apple Shortcut that copies a note from Apple Notes and opens this Import page automatically:
  1. Open Shortcuts app on iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap + → Add Action → Get Notes (from Notes app)
  3. Add action: Get Text from Note
  4. Add action: Copy to Clipboard
  5. Add action: Open URLs → paste your Roots & Records URL
  6. Save shortcut as "Send to Roots & Records"
  7. Add it to your Home Screen for one-tap access
Then just paste the copied text into the Import box above.
